## Districts Overview

### Southwest District (Most chapters: ~95)
Major concentration in Texas and Oklahoma
Notable schools: UT Austin, Oklahoma State, Texas Tech, Baylor, SMU

### Southeast District (~85 chapters)
Strong presence in traditional Southern states
Includes most HBCU chapters
Notable schools: Florida State, Auburn, Georgia, Alabama

Heavy presence in Ohio, Michigan, Indiana
Notable schools: Ohio State, Michigan, Purdue, Indiana

Pacific Coast and Mountain states
Notable schools: Stanford, UCLA, USC, Washington

Great Plains states
Notable schools: Nebraska, Kansas State, Iowa State

### Northeast District (~28 chapters)
Mid-Atlantic and New England
